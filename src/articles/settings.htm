From the main menu you can navigate to the Settings page:

In ModoQuiz settings you have the following options:

Change profile photo

Simply tap the image in the circle and follow the instructions on the screen.

Enable / Disable Notifications

By default, ModoQuiz notifies you under the following circumstances:

  • When a challenge is going to expire — challenge expiration period is 24 hours

  • When you are challenged

  • Every round when it is your turn

Turn sound on/off

Change username

5 symbols or more, maximum - 20 symbols. No spaces allowed. Must be unique.

Change your full name

At least 5 symbols, maximum - 100 symbols

Log out

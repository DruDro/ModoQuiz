On Modo Quiz launch, you need to log in to the application. Tap the area shown on the screenshot below:

You have several options to continue with:

  • Log in using an existing account

  • Create a new Modo Quiz account

  • Log in using a Facebook account

Log in using an existing account

If you already have an existing Modo Quiz account, type in your USERNAME and PASSWORD and tap LOGIN

Create a new Modo Quiz account

Tap CREATE NEW ACCOUNT and follow the steps:

  • Choose a usernamerequired field — 4 characters or more, maximum - 256 characters. Must be unique

  • Enter your emailrequired field — '@' and '.' are required characters, maximum - 256 characters

  • Enter your full namerequired field — at least 1 character, maximum - 256 characters

  • Enter a passwordrequired field — 6 characters or more ( minimum 5 any symbols and at least 1 number), maximum - 256 characters

  • Choose your language from the drop-down list

  • START PLAYING

Log in using a Facebook account

You can use your Facebook account to log in to Modo Quiz. Tap SIGN IN WITH FACEBOOK. On the appeared screen you can choose whether to use the Facebook app installed on your device or to log in to Facebook using a browser.

After you confirm signing in with Facebook, you need to choose a username for Modo Quiz.

Your profile photo will be attached to the newly created Modo Quiz account. You can change it whenever you want. For this, tap the photo and follow the inctructions on the screen.

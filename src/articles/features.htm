Multiple languages

Modo Quiz doesn't limit you with one language - while signing up to Modo Quiz, you can select a comfortable language from the list. Or do it later on the SETTINGS page

Play One On One or with multiple players

There are two quiz modes available in Modo Quiz: ONE ON ONE and CHALLENGE.

In the ONE ON ONE mode, you will compete with a single opponent for 3 rounds, 6 questions each.

In the CHALLENGE mode, any number of players can join a challenge, if you invite them to take part in a quiz. There are no rounds in this mode: players answer all questions in one go.

Facebook integration, quiz sharing, making friends

Modo Quiz allows and encourages you to post quiz invitations to your Facebook wall or directly to your friends. You can add friends both within the game, if you know the username of a person, or invite friends from Facebook. The more you share and make friends the more rewards you get.

Custom questions and topics

There is a huge list of preset categories of questions in Modo Quiz. Any sphere of life, culture, general knowledge, etc. can be selected to start a quiz. But, moreover, you can create your own topics and questions via the Modo Quiz Admin Panel. Be original!

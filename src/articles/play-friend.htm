When you choose to challenge a friend, next you should select a friend from the list of friends you have added earlier.

If you want to challenge more than one friend, you can follow the steps descibed in the Challenge Friends article.

Then you will get to the SELECT TOPIC screen, where, obviously, a topic and a subcategory should be selected from the variety of topics.

In Modo Quiz you can select a topic from the list of preset topics, but also create a custom topic with your questions. See more details in the Custom Questions article.

As soon as you select a subtopic, you get to the Quiz screen - and the quiz begins.

This is a one-on-one quiz. There are 3 rounds of the quiz. 6 questions in each round. When a round is complete, the player who initiated the challenge has to wait until the opponent completes the round.

See the How to Play -> Quiz gameplay section for more details on how to play and use hints.

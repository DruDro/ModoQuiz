General Info

A quiz game will let friends find out who can answer the quiz faster. Players should be fast and clever enough to answer quickly and to answer correctly.

You can go further and purchase the quiz aids which will help you win!

There is no geographical barriers as you can play the ModoQuiz game using different languages.

Make sure to check your Facebook and find out whether your friends are already playing the game - you will be surprised to find them there. Otherwise just go and invite them all to play the ModoQuiz today!

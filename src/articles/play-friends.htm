When you choose to challenge friends, next you should give the challenge a name (at least 6 symbols) and tap START YOUR CHALLENGE.

Then you will be suggested to post the challenge to Facebook. Anyone from your Facebook friends will be able to join the challenge.

Then you will get to the SELECT TOPIC screen, where, obviously, a topic and a subcategory should be selected from the variety of topics.

In Modo Quiz you can not only select from the predefined topic, but also create a custom topic with your questions. See more details in the Categories article.

As soon as you select a subtopic, you get to the Quiz screen - and the quiz begins.

This is a challenge quiz. There are no rounds and, respectively, no need to wait for an opponent to complete a quiz round. Just answer all quensions in a row in the given time.

See the Quiz article for more details on how to play and use hints.
